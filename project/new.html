About NAC

NUAMES Athletic Club (NAC) is the governing body for sports at Nuames. It was created by Angie Mendieta and Sarah Aliche and it is the first

sports club created at Nuames. We are both sophmores and our goal is to expand our club as much as possible. NAC encompasses more than 7

sports and multiple events. Signing up is easy, just contact the email in the footer below and let us know if you're interested in joining. You can

also join the remind app

Remind app text: @bbahea to the number: 81010 to join the app. Don't forget to invite your friends. Feel free to join at anytime; there is

no deadline. However, you must pay to join. More information can be founnd in the "Prices and Merchandise" page :)

Mission Statement

NAC was created to develop and promote the ideals of responsibility, hardwork, sportsmanship, and teamwork within a safe and positive environment.

What We Do

  • Promoting a program of sports for boys and girls at Nuames

  • Establishing and enforcing athletics

  • Sanctioning multiple events and more than 7 sports

  • Providing the opportunity for students who are interested in sports to do so here

  • Engaging students in not only academics but athletics
